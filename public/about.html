About CyberGuard Africa

CyberGuard Africa is a youth-led digital safety initiative founded to empower Africans through cybersecurity education, awareness, and innovation. The project aims to create a generation of informed and responsible internet users who can protect themselves and others in the online world.

Established by a passionate young visionary, CyberGuard Africa focuses on bridging the gap between technology and safety — especially among students and young professionals. Through campaigns, online learning, and mentorship, the initiative seeks to build a culture of cyber awareness across African communities.

Our Mission

To empower African youth with cybersecurity knowledge and digital skills that enable them to stay safe, innovate responsibly, and lead in the global digital space.

Our Goals

  • Promote digital safety awareness in schools and communities.
  • Encourage young Africans to explore cybersecurity careers.
  • Provide free and accessible cyber education resources.
  • Collaborate with partners to fight online scams and fraud.
